1) driter festival fun yidishe lider, netanya zumer 1994.
   tzulib tekhnishe sibes, iz di program opgeleygt oyf tzvey vokhn.
   ---> di naye date iz: dem 14tn oygust, 1994. 8:00 ovnt, amfiteater
        gan ah melekh, netanya.
1) Third Festival of Yiddish songs, Netanya summer, 1994.
   For technical reasons, the program has been moved forward by two weeks.
   ---> The new date is teh 14th of August, 1994, 8:00 evening,
        Amphitheatre, "GAN HAMELEKH", Netanya.
2) tzvey gor talentirte kinstler, mitglider funem hemshekh dor veln zayn
   in amerike in gikhn:
2) Two very talented artists, members of the HEMSHEKH DOR will be in the US:
   RUT LEVIN - zingerin, vet oyftretn in dem zumer lager funem arbeter ring.
               zi vet zayn dort funem 17tn yuli biz dem 15tn oygust. zi vet
               nokh zayn in noy-york biz sof september.
               RUT iz di tokhter funem bakantn yidishn retzitator/compozitor
               LEYBU LEVIN, fun tshernovitz.
               RUT iz a profesionele zingerin un hot gezungen oyf a sakh
               kontzertn in yisroyl un in eyrope.  zi hot zayer a raykhn
               repertuar fun lider oyf yidish. (oykh a spetziele program fun
               ir tatns verk). es iz kday ir tzu geyn hern.
               Zi volt geven tzufridn oyftzutretn oykh far andere grupes un
               organizatzies . men ken ir kontaktirn durkhn kultur opteylung
               funem arbeter ring in noy-york.
   RUTH LEVIN, singer, will perform in the Workmen's Circle summer resort .
               She will be there from the 17th of July to the 15th of August.
               She will then be in New York until the end of September.
               RUTH is the daughter of the well known Yiddish recitor/composer
               LEYBU LEVIN, of Tchernowicz.
               RUTH is a professional singer and has sung in many concerts in
               Israel and in Europe.  She has a very rich repertoir of Yiddish
               songs. (Also a special program of her father's works). It is
               worthwhile to go hear her.
               She would be happy to perform for other organizations and groups,
               and can be reached through the NY Workmen's Circle Cultural Dept.

   HAIYA LIVNI - fidlerin, vet zayn oyf a tur fun kontzertn mit dem yisroyldikn
               kameri orkester, funem 7tn oktober biz dem 4tn november.
               a khutz ir raykhn repertuar fun klasishe muzik, git HAIYA a
               sheynem solo program fun yidishe artistish-gaystishe nigunim
               un folks melodyes. dos vert bagleyt mit fartaytshungen un
               oyfklerungen oyf yidish un english.  di fraye dates vos zi ken
               oyftretn zaynen dem 28stn oktober, un november 1,2 un 3, 1994.
               ver es iz farinteresirt in ir program, zol zi shraybn oyf
               dem vayterdikn adres: HAIYA LIVNI, 34 SIRKIN, TEL AVIV 63562.
               (oder zol mir shikn an elektre-briv mit zeyer adres/telefon,
               vel ikh es ir ibergebn --- leybl).
   HAIYA LIVNI - Violinist, will be on a concert tour with the Israel Chamber
               Orchestra from October 7th until November 4th.
               Besides her rich Classical repertoire, HAIYA has a beautiful
               solo program of Yiddish and Jewish artistic and spiritual music
               and folks melodies.  She accompanies this with explanations and
               commentary in Yiddish and in English.  Her free dates that she
               can perform are the 28th of October and November 1,2 & 3, 1994.
               Anyone interested in her program should write her at the
               following address: HAIYA LIVNI, 34 SIRKIN, TEL AVIV 63562.
               (or send me an e-mail letter with their address/telephone,
               and I'll pass it along --- Leybl).

leybl botvinik
