1) A poem by Leyb Kvitko (Bob Rothstein)

Date: Thu Nov 11 23:36:34 1993
From: ROBERT A ROTHSTEIN <rar@slavic.umass.edu>
Subject: A poem by Leyb Kvitko

	Since Mendele has been silent of late, I hereby offer a
favorite poem by the Soviet writer Leyb Kvitko for those who are
raising Yiddish-speaking children (and for anyone else who might
enjoy it).  I actually first read it in Samuil Marshak's Russian
translation.  (In the interest of saving space, I've put the last
ten stanzas in a second column.)


               Dos ferdl fun banakht

Gekumen tsu forn               Firt Maksl dos ferdl,
der pape banakht,              di mame er vekt.
er hot fun der rayze           Er geyt tsu tsum pape,
a ferdl gebrakht.              er rayst im di dek.

A ferdl aza mins,              Zey zaynen farshlofn
vi Maks hot gevolt:            un entfern koym:
der kop un di tloen            --Me zet nit dos ferdl!
mit shvartsn gemolt.           Me zet nit di tsoym!

Nit poshet mit shvartsn,       Iz firt er es vayter,
nor take lakirt,               Zayn ferdele firt,
un take dos tsayml             un klapt mit a hentl
mit zilber batsirt.            dem shokhn in tir.

Aza min vil Maksl              Se ligt nokh der shokhn,
shoyn zeyer fun lang,          fun bet fregt er:  --Ver?
ot shteyt es, dos ferdl,       --Ikh vil dir bavayzn,
aleyn af der bank.             Bavayzn mayn ferd.

Un kukt zikh af Makslen,       --Ikh hob es gezen...
se vart un se kukt,            Mayn tir iz farmakht.
nor Maksl veyst gornit,        --Du host nit gezen,
er shloft un genug!            s'a nays fun banakht!

Fartog ersht derzet er,        --Ikh ken es, dayn ferdl,
tseshrayt zikh nit shir:       ikh ken es genug.
--Se dakht zikh, a ferdl       --Du kenst es nit, kenst nit.
farlofn tsu mir!               Ot tu nor a kuk!

Vos iz es, a kholem            --Ikh zog dir, ikh ken es,
tsi take a ferd?               ot her, az ikh ken:
Er raybt zikh di oygn          Eyn kop hot dayn ferdl.
un kukt alts un klert.         --Vi veystu es den?

Un bald mit di hentlekh        --Her vayter, dayn ferdl
a freydikn patsh:              fir fislekh farmogt!
--Se gor nit keyn kholem!      --Fir fislekh?  Fir fislekh!
S'a gantser ferdatsh!          Vi veystu es, zog?

Arop un gekhapt es             Farkhidesht iz Maksl,
un tut es a fir,               farkhidesht un klert:
a tsiter tut Maksl             "Er hot nit gezen
ba yedn zayn rir.              un ken es, mayn ferd!

A kop -- iz a kop,             Vi kon er es kenen?
un an ek -- iz an ek!          Az s'iz gor ba mir!"
Bakukn di fislekh              Un Maks, a farklerter,
er leygt zikh avek.            kukt shtil af der tir.

Se geyt, az me shlept es!
Se shteyt, az men vil!
Un oyb es tseleygn,
Iz ligt es dir shtil.

	Bob Rothstein
