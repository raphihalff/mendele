What's wrong with Weinreich's College Yiddish? It is probably
available in most libraries, and even if you have no background, you
can probably get going in it as rapidly, certainly as
systematically, as in any other way. I suppose it would be more
supportive, as well as more fun, to do it in a group with a few
other folks.

Subject: Introduction

My name is Mark Flumerfelt and I'm a graduate student at York
University in Tor onto.  I'm presently doing my comprehensives but I
will be writing my dissertat ion on some aspect of Canadian Jewish
history.  I am particularly interested in Jewish socialist and
labour organizations in the 1930's (ie-Left Poale Zion). I am
presently learning Yiddish (mit a lehrer) and as a result I have
become fascinated with Yiddish literature.  I am particularly
interested in the works of Chaim Grade and I.J. Singer.  I'll be
glad to discuss these authors with any one who is interested.

PS - Has anyone heard of a Yiddish author from Montreal named
Zipper?  Just curious.

Mark Flumerfelt

Subject: yiddish music & di yiddishe gas

Regarding Hope Ehn's search for Yiddish music, the Jewish Book
Center of the Workmen's Circle sells a large variety of Yiddish and
Klezmer tapes, cds and songbooks. The Israel Book Shop in Brookline
is the exception to the rule. Most Judaica shops do not have such a
large variety of Yiddish recordings. For more information, please
call 212-889-6800 ext.285 or 800-922-2885 ext.285.

We have something new in the Jewish Book Center that may be of
interest to Mendelniks. A new Yiddish journal is being published in
Moscow with the help of the Forward and the Committee for the
Revitalization of Yiddish in the Soviet Union called "Di Yiddishe
Gas". The journal contains stories, articles, poems, memoirs and
correspondence from expatriates in Israel. If you are interested in
purchasing a copy, please contact the Jewish Book Center of the
Workmen's Circle.

Stephen Dowling

From: Martin  Lakin <mlakin@acpub.duke.edu>
Subject: Kol Korei bamidbar far hilf fun Mendelnikes

Taiyere Mendelnikes, Gor koortz geredt, hoben mir do in Durham,
N.C.und in Chapel Hill (oich in Raleigh hoben mir a por mentschen) a
Yiddisher Kreiz. Tzuvishen unz zeinen die velche kenen gor gut reden
und leyenen, und oich hoben mir azelche velche kenen nisht leyenen
Yiddishe shrift und darfen ubertzetzen  (transliterate) kedei zei
zollen kenen leyenen. Trotz dem, zeinen alle mutig und shtoltz die
mitglider fun unzer kreiz. Mistameh zeinen a por Mendelnikes bakant
mit unzer Shevah Zucker. Durch ihr initziativ hoben mir zich
tzuzamen gekumen.

Alzo, vil ich aich betten a tovah: Vi a mol leyenen mir gor ernste
zachen, und avadeh leyent men Sholem Aleichemen. Ober vos iz takeh
gut is az men leyent aza komische zach vi Monger's Megillah Lider
und yeder shpilt ainer fun die Megillah loit, tzum baishpil, Vashti,
oder Haman der roishe, oder Estershe, oder Pastrigoso, der shneider
yung. Dan iz yeder tzufreiden, Ainer helft dem tzveiten in zein
rolye, und tzuzamen greiten mir zich vi tzu an emeser forshtellung.
Der noch heren mir zich tzu tzu a tashme fun der forshtelling vos iz
forgekumen in Tel Aviv in 1966. Vos iz der sof? Yeder iz kemat
baztzeigt az er iz takeh a groisser shoispiller und es art im nisht
vos er ken gor vainig Yiddish!

Ober ganz ernst gerredt, die mentschen vellen keinmol nisht
farinteressiren zich in Yiddish Linguistics, nor ez is zei zeyer
noent und tzart, veil es dermont zei vegen kindveiss, oder vegent
die fargangene Bobes und Zeides, die tates und mames, b'emes iz dos
Nostalgia. Ober fun Nostalgia ken a sach vaksen.

Ober meine libe Mendelnikes, vos bet ich fun aiyich? Az ihr zolt
bald kumen tzu gehn unz rateven fun unzer golles? Chas v'sholem! Mir
rekehenen az mir veinen in der Yerushalayim fun der dorom. Iher kent
unz takeh helfen ven ihr shikt tzu oder dermont unz vegen pastlache
material far azelche forshtellungen vie die Megillah Lide. Men darf
ober hoben in zihnen az unzer mitglider zeinen hoich gebildete
"universitetnikes"(azoi vie ihr alleh, nor zeyer fayikeitten in
Yiddish zeinen zeyer bagrenitzt (achutz Shevah, Harav Schoen fun
Raleigh, a gor merkvediker Yid, Menachem (Max) Drucker, velcher iz
takeh geven a printer in a Yiddishe Presse, und mein veib, Mussie
(zol gezundt zein) )

Alzo, oib ihr hot Got in hartzen (vie mein bobeh flegt zogen) Nemt
die fis oif die plaitzes, und zukht far unz in ayire shkafen und
bicher shranken. Efsher hot der Arbeiter Ring oder die Sholem
Aleichem schulen gedrukt azelche material far kinder. Ich bet ayich
az ihr zolt mir tzushiken ayire aitzes. Ich vart umgeduldig oif
entvers tzu mein "Kol Korei Bamidbar".

Mottel Lakin a.k.a. Mottel der operator
(In certain quarters referred to as Mottke Ganif)
