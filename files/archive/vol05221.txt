Subject: Needed: biographical information

Aliza Greenblatt, Elkhonen Indelman, Ben Yomen, Leon Wajner

Mir shteln tsunoyf a bikhl  kinderlider mit tsugob informatsye vegn di poetn
un  kompozitorn.  Es feln undz teyl faktn. Tsi veyst emitser  vegn di folgnde
shrayber, poetn, pedagogn un kompozitorn?

1.  Aliza Grinblat hot geshribn lider far groys un kleyn.  Zi iz geboyrn
gevorn in 1888.  Ven iz zi geshtorbn?

2.  Elkhonen Indelman iz geven a yidisher dertziyer un oykh geshribn far
kinder af yidish un hebreish.  Er iz geboyrn gevorn in 1913.  Ven iz er
geshtorbn?

3.  Ben Yomen iz geven a kompozitor un muzik lerer in di amerikaner shuln un
zumerkemps.  Vos nokh veyst men vegn im ?

4.  Leon Wajner/Wainer/ Weiner  iz geven a kompozitor vos iz avek fun Poyln
keyn Argentine.  Vos nokh veyst men vegn im ?

A dank in foroys .

We are compiling a book of children's songs with additional information about
their  lyricists and composers .  However, we are missing some information.
Does anyone know about the following poets, composers and educators?

1  Aliza Greenblatt wrote songs for young and old. She was born in 1888. When
did she die?

2.  Elkhonen Indelman was a Jewish educator and wrote for children in Yiddish
and Hebrew.  He was born in 1913.  When did he die?

3.  Ben Yomen was a composer and music teacher who worked in the American
secular Yiddish schools and summercamps. What else is known about him?

4.  Leon Wajner/Wainer/Weiner is/was a composer who left Poland for Argentina.
What else is known about him ?

Your help would be greatly appreciated.

Perl Teitelbaum
Queens, New York
