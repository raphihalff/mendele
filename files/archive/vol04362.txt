Yugntruf Aktivitet Reshime.....................................

Zenen do bahaltene aktiorn zvishn aykh?  Efsher azelekhe vos bakumen
nisht genug gelegnheytn tsu bavayzn di dramatishe talantn? Viln mir aykh
farbetn mitsushpiln mit undzerer _Shmueskrayz_ vu mir veln... Shpontan
Repetirn un Oyffirn Yidishe Purim Shpiln! S'vet nisht zayn key
aktiorn-forher!  K'dey tsu zayn eyne fun di Purim-Spilers, darft ir nor
kenen leynen Yidish.  K'dey tsu zayn in oylem darft ir nor visn vi azoy
tsu zitsn un aplodirn.  Un ven me darf lakhn.  Di gor hanoadike
Purim-Spil vet men forshteln Montik, dem 13tn Marts, 7:00 in ovnt in
Daytshes-Hoyz, 420 vest 116te gas, Manhetn.  (Nemt di unterban #1 oder di
oytobusn M4/M104 biz 116ter gas un Brodvey.  Geyt 1-1/2 gasn oyf mizrakh
aribern kolombie- kampus.)  Me vet derlangen homen tashn un nor Purimdikn
kibud. Entfert zeyt moykhl, bizn 12tn Marts: Brukhe Lang, (212) 280-1168.
Arayngang: Yugntruf-mitglider $2; nisht mitglider $3, studentn $1.

                         **************

Der Sholem-Aleykhem-Kultur-Tsenter farbet aykh oyf zeyer Purim- Simkhe
mit di prekhtike zingers Susan Goldberg un Peter Shlosser. Dertsu vet
Tsharne Schaechter firleynen _Purim_ fun Sholem- Aleichem.  Di gor
spetsiele Purim-Simkhe vet firkumen Zuntik dem 1stn Marts, 1:00n"m, in
2201 Bainbridge Avenue, Bronx.  (Nemt di ban #4 tsu Mosholu Parkway, oder
di "D" biz 205ter gas, un spatsir tsu tsu 208ter gas un Bainbridge Ave.)
Me vet derlangen Purimdike kibud.  Arayngang: Yugntruf-mitglider umzist;
nisht mitglider $2.50.

                         **************

Shryabkrayz: donershtik dem 23stn Marts, 6:00n"m in byuro fun Yugntruf.

Gib a hilf di nay ongekumener tsu der Yidish redndik velt!!!! Farshprey
di nayes mit di vos ir kenen vos lernen Yidish. Undzer Shmueskrayz far
onheybers treft zikh Zuntig dem 26stn Marts, 2:00n"m in Daytshes Hoyz
(zet adres iber). Entfert bizn 24stn Martz: Brukhe, (212) 208-1168.  Me
vet derlangen kibud.  Arayngang: Student/Yugntruf umzist; nisht mitglid

Gedenk!  Di naye muzikalishe revi _Too Jewish?_ not zeyer oysgenumen, hot
me zi farlengert bizn 18tn Yuni.

                         **************

Mendelnikes:   Vi dermont Leybl, der merhayt fun ot di aktivitetn kumen
for in der New Yorker gegnt.  Mir derkenen az a sakh fun aykh voynen gor
vayt fun New York.  Mir hofn az ir kenen opnemen gedanken far aktivitetn
far Yugntruf (un Yugntruf min) grupes in ayere voyn erter.  Oyb ir hot
frages vegn vi azoy tsu firn ot der oder der ander aktivitet farbindt
zikh mit undz.  Vayter...gedenk vos dos zenen nor di mitl yorike
aktivitetn fun Yugntruf.  Yedn zumer (arum "Labor Day") kumt for undzer
prekhtige _Yidish=Vokh_.  Di Yidish=Vokh treft zikh in a sheyne valdike
kolonie in Massachusetts.  A gantse vokh farbrengt men, me rut zikh op,
me shpatzirt zikh, me shpilt...un me hert, shmuest un redt Yidish.  Af
der _Yidish=Vokh_ kumen Yugntruf mitglider fun New York vi mairiv Canade
un Ayrope.

Are there hidden actors amoung you who don't get enough of a chance to
act?  Hams amoung your (kosher, of course) who don't get enough of a
chance to ham?  Audience-ish folk amoung you who don't get enough of a
chance to aud?  Then come join our Yiddish discussion circle as we
spontaneously rehearse and perform Yiddish Purim plays!  No Auditions!
In order to be one of the performers, you only need to know how to read
Yiddish.  In dorder to be one of the audience, you only need to know how
to sit and applaud.  And when to laugh.  This jolly-good-Purim-fun
theatrical event will take place on Mon., Mar. 13, 7:00pm at Deutshes
Haus, 420 West 116th Street, Manhattan.  (Take the #1 train or the
M4/M104 bus to 116 Street and Broadway, and walk 1-1/2 blocks east
through the Columbia campus.)  RSVP by Sun., Mar. 12 to Brukhe Lang,
(212) 280- 1168.  Homen-tashn and other refreshments will be served.
Admission: Yugntrufers $2; non-members $3; students $1.

                            *********
