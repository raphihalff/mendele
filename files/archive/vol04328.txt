Subject: Yidishe farlagn

Far Meylekh Viswanath un ale vos farinteresirn zikh mit yidishe farlagn
un farlegers:

Ikh hob geshribn mayn master's teze vegn yidishe kultur in Berlin tsvishn
di yorn 1919 un 1925, ober der iker vegn yidishe varlagn un farlegers vos
hobn zikh bazetst af a por yorn in yener shtot.  Zey hobn publikirt in di
yorn _a sakh_ bikher un zshurnaln, lemoshl der sheyner literarisher
kinstlekher zshournal _Milgroym_, radaktirt fun Mark Wischnitzer un
Rachel Wischnitzer-Bernstein.  Men hot gekent oysnitsn di umshtandn in
daytshland, vi di shreklekhe inflatsye, tsu produtsirn a sakh sheyne
bikher un zshurnaln zeyer bilik, bikher vos me hot gedarft in in
mizrakh-eyrope, der iker in di shuln.

Eyn teyl fun mayn master's teze iz a reshime fun ale titlen vos me hot
aroysgegebn in Berlin in di yorn.  Ikh shrayb itst an artikel vegn di
farleger-aktivitetn, a hemshekh fun yene arbet fun Leo un Renate Fuks
(Leo Baeck Institute Yearbook 1988).  Az emetster farinteresirt zikh mit
di titel-reshime, oder mit mayn master's teze (vos iz af daytsh!), volt
ikh gekent shikn a kopye.

Glenn Levine

Subject: Yugntruf af Yidish!

Tayerer Leybl un der Mendele reshime,

Leybl, a sheynem dank daynem "ofenem briv" af Mendele tsu Yugntruf.  Ikh
fil zikh yoysher dir an entfer tsu gebn vayl ikh bin der vos iz geyheysn
shikn undzere Yugntruf New Yorker aktivitetn ariber tsu Mendele.

Mir veysn az nisht nor du yoynst gor vayt (Un vos iz vayt in undzere
hayntike velt?) fun der shtodt New York un kenst nisht (vi a katz)
shpringen keyn New York vi du vilst :).  Ober faran di vos kumen fun
tsayt tsu tsayt af nesies - afile glaykh ven es kumt for epes Yidish.

Nokh vayter kenen di aktivitetn gebn gedanken tsu andere aktivitetn far
ayer "Yugntruf opteylungen" oyser New York (es volt efsher dermonen in
meshiakhn)... in New York afile khapn mir gedanken far aktivitetn fun
alerley erter.

Vegn shraybn af Yidish: Ikh bin dir gor maskim az vos mer mir kenen redn
un shraybn af Yidish vos beser.  Ikh nem es tsum hartzn un ikh vel es
pruvn tun (pruvn Ruvn) far di kumendike aktivitetn reshimes.

Zol zayn mit glik!

Ruvn Millman

Subject: Third International Yiddish Club Conference

The Committee has had discussions with six cities and had narrowed it
down to three. In the last several days serious meetings and long-
distance calls has narrowed down the selection, and an announcement will
soon be made.

The Committee consists of a representative from each of the four Yiddish
Club newsletters in North America, the moderator of the Mendele list and
an independent Yiddish activist.

It is hoped to be held in early 1996 and in the United States.

Phil "Fishl" Kutner

Subject: Shraybkrayz

_Der Bay_ is a monthly Anglo-Yiddish newsletter which compiles lists of
Yiddish teachers, Yiddish clubs, Klezmer bands, etc., and has periodic
requests. One such request came in recently.

"We have leyenkrayzn, shmoozkrayzn and a shraybkrayz. The only
shraybkrayz in the States of which I am aware is sponsored by Yugntruf in
NY."

There appears to be interest in forming one--possibly online or through
snail mail :- ).

If another one exists, or if you wish to join one, please let me
know--especially if you wish to form a leyenkrayz, please contact me
at:
