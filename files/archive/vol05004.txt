Subject: Yidish-Vokh: di gantse megile

Yugnt far Yidish
20ste yerlekhe yidish-vokh

*di pyonirishe Yidish-vakatsie*

di vokh fun Arbeter-tog, Mitvokh, dem 30stn oygust, biz mitvokh, dem 6tn
oktober 1995 in kemp Emanuel, Kopayk, New York.  In di prekhtike berkshir
berg.

ma nishtana - far vos iz under Yidish-Vokh andersh fun ale andere
Yidish-aroysforn?....

Af ale andere Yidish-oryentirte aroysforn redt men y English, y Yidish -
der iker English.  Ober oyf Yugntruf yerlekher Yidish-Vokh bamien mir
zikh _tsu redn nor yidish_, tsi ir kent Yidish fun der heym tsi fun a klas,
tsi ayer Yidish-redn iz flisikh tsi tsebrokhn, "beser a tsebrokhn Yidish
vi a gut English."  iz kent ir esn un trinkn, zikh vitslen, zikh kitslen,
zikh kulien, shet hulien, zikh broynen, zikh brenen - alts af Yidish.

Af ale andere Yidish-aroysforn darft ir oder tsoln $500-$600 a mentsh a
vokh, oder kent ir tsoln veyniker ober derbay kent ir nisht farbrengen
mer vi a kurtsn sof-vokh.  af yungrufs yerlekher yidish-vokh bkumt ir an
umgloyblekh vilde metsie!!!  ir kent blaybn gantse zibn teg far _bloyz 180
tolar_, arayngerekhnt trinkgelt un transport fun der shtot new york!!
dertsu zenen do spetsiele hanokhes far kaledj-studentn arbet-shtudirnikes.

Af ale andere Yidish-aroysforn zenen do a tuts klasn in der fri, nokh a
tuts klasn bay tog un nokh mer in ovnt, az me ken azsh nisht khapn dem
otem.  af Yugntrufs yerlekher Yidish-Vokh felt nisht keyn gelegnhayt zikh
oystsushpanen, tsu khapn a shmues un zikh tsu bakenen mit naye khaveyrim.
di Yidish-Vokh iz far ale vos zukhn a hanoadike iberlebung mit netsbol,
koyshbol, shvimen, shiflen zikh, zingen, tantsn, natur-shatsirn,
lager-fayern, Yidishe filmen, talantarnies, televizie-farmest-shpiln azoy
vi "dzshepardi", un lektsies gehaltene fun bavuste keners fun der
Yidisher shprakh un kultur vi, lemoshl, Dr. Sheva Zucker, Dr. Shmuel
Kassow, Dr. Mordkhe Schaechter - alts af yidish!!

**"Eyn vokh fun yor - an emese Yidish-Velt - Biz 20 Yor!!!"**

dos esn iz glat kosher un s'iz do a shul.  s'vet zayn a Yidish-redndike
kindhiterin bshas gevise aktivitetn.  di kinder veln zikh vayln bay
sport, handtarbet un kunst, un oytser-geyog, pyeskelekh un nokh un nokh.

afile der nakhes vos ir shept vet zayn af Yidish!!

Prayzn bizn 20stn Yuli (in amerikaner tolarn):

   $180 a mentsh - tsvay in a tsimer, teyln zikh mit a vashtsimer
   $220 a mentsh - tsvay in a tsimer, privater vashtsimer,
      yokhid teylt zikh mit a vashtsimer
   $310 - yokhid, privater tsimer un vashtsimer

kinder biz dray yor kumen umzist.  kinder dray yor un elter tsoln dem fuln
prayz.  oyb ir rgistrirt zikh nokhn 20stn yuli, darft ir tsutsoln nokh $20 a
mentsh.  ale bateylikte in der Yidish-Vokh darfn zayn Yugntruf-Mitglider.

dos khaver-gelt iz ober zay vi a metsie:  $10 far studentn, $18 far
nisht-studentn.

Nokh vayterdike protim kent ir shraybn in byro fun Yugnruf (200 vest 72ste
gas, #40,  New York, NY  10023-2824) oder klingen (212-787-6675), oder sikhn
mit der elektronisher post tsu Ruvn Millman: ruvn@aol.com.

gedenkt zshe tsu rezervirn ayere erter BALT.  shikt arayn aplikir-boygn, in
eynem mit ayer tshek (in amerikaner tolarn, oysgeshtelt tsu Yugntruf) in byro
un Yugntruf (adres do oybn).

Subject: Kreitzik

I am interested in the origin of the word _kreitzik_. An aunt of mine
said that her mother owuld use this word to refer to the heel of a loaf
of bread. I know, however, that this word refers to the crucifixion or
simply a cross.  WHat, if any, is the connection?

Subject: Koyletsh

Russians bake a kuleetsh (emphasis on the second syllable) at Easter: in
the variation that I have tasted it is a dense, sweet, rich coffee cake
that makes one think of challah plus 2 pounds of butter, sugar, and eggs,
traditionally (I am told) round and high, and not to be confused with the
Russian kalatsh, a less rich but silmilar cake prepared for name days and
other family celebrations.

David Goldberg
